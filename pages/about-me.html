About me

Hi! I am just some guy and I am interested in stuff. All opinion expressed on this site are purely my own and do not reflect the opinion of my work, my family or my non-existent dog.

I studied computer science at the DHBW in Stuttgart and the Technische Universität in Berlin. I now work a the Quality and Usability Lab and I am trying to do my PhD there.

I hope this blog will be a mixture of technical and non-technical stuff. Stay tuned.
